HTML Entities ♥

You're probably familiar with them, maybe you've seen &amp; on a website and wondered what is was

HTML entities serve two purposes:

  1. They allow for you to use characters which are normally probited in content by HTML (such as the < symbol)
  2. They acted as a proto-emoji in the early days of the net
